Three Column Layout

Column One

This is the first column of your three-column layout. You can add any content you want here, such as text, images, or other HTML elements.

The columns will automatically adjust based on the screen size. On smaller screens, they will stack vertically.

Column Two

This is the second column of your layout. Each column takes up an equal amount of space in the container.

These columns use CSS flexbox for layout, which makes them flexible and responsive to different screen sizes.

Column Three

This is the third column of your layout. You can customize the colors, spacing, and other styles in the CSS section.

Feel free to edit this template to match your website's design and content needs.
